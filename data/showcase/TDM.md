<h2>Map objectives</h2>

<ul>
<li>Team 5x5</li>
<li>Asymmetric</li>
</ul>

I chose a factory setting for the narrative to allow flexibility in designing high ground (HG) and the overall environment.

<hr>

<h2>Design</h2>

I decided to emphasize the right-side high ground (HG) and balance the rest of the map to reduce the advantage of that spot.

To achieve this, I did:

<ul>
<li>A high ground position opposite it, which the opposing team can reach faster</li>
<li>An exit route for the opposing team through an almost hidden flank, allowing them to approach the spot from behind unnoticed (due to the protruding shape of the high ground)</li>
<li>Covered areas to balance line of sight (vision)</li>
</ul>

<h3>Item design (Shields, Medkits, Ammo)</h3>

Ammo is placed so that players have to leave active combat points, but not too far, allowing them to reposition strategically.

<a href="/assets/Portfolio/Personal_works/TDM/Screenshot_519.png" data-lightbox="refs" data-title="Refs">
  <img src="/assets/Portfolio/Personal_works/TDM/Screenshot_519.png">
</a>

Medkits are placed in riskier areas — they can be reached relatively stealthily (especially from the high ground), but must be picked up quickly (so players can’t hold the position for long)
